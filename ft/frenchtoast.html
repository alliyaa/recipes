Ingredients

  1. Eggs

  2. Brioche Bread

  3. Whipping Cream

  4. Condensed Milk

  5. Cooking Oil

  6. Brown and White Sugar

  7. Milk



Recipe

  1. To make the batter, add 1 cup of milk in a bowl

  2. Add 2 eggs in the bowl

  3. Add a 1/2 a cup of white sugar and 1/8 a cup of brown sugar

  4. Add a 1/4 cup of whipping cream

  5. Get a brioche bread and bathe it in the batter

  6. Take another bowl and fill with 1/4 cup of condensed milk

  7. Dip the brioche bread in the batter and then in the condensed
    milk then again in the batter.

  8. Grease the pain with oil, fry the french toast
